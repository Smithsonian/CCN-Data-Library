Dataset: Contributions of Organic and Mineral Matter to Vertical Accretion in Tidal Wetlands across a Chesapeake Bay Subestuary

Authors: Jenny R. Allen <jennyrallen@gmail.com>, Jeffrey C. Cornwell, and Andrew H. Baldwin.

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.25573/serc.18130892

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in four separate .csv files which can by joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

allen_et_al_2022_materials_and_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
allen_et_al_2022_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
allen_et_al_2022_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
allen_et_al_2022_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.

metadata.xml - Contains a full suite of descriptive metadata with all attribute and variables defined, units clarified, and geographic, temporal, and taxonomic coverages described according to Ecological Metadata Language Standards.

metadata.html - Is a simplified, visual and interactive version of metadata.xml for display purposes.

allen_et_al_2022_associated_publication.bib - Contains bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
