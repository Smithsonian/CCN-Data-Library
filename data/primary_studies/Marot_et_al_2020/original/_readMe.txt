Dataset: Sedimentary Data from Grand Bay, Alabama/Mississippi, 2014

Authors: Marci E. Marot, Christopher G. Smith, Terrence A. McCloskey, Stanley D. Locker, Nicole S. Khan, Kathryn E.L. Smith

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.5066/P9FO8R3Y


The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in four separate .csv files which can by joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Marot_et_al_2020_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study
Marot_et_al_2020_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations
Marot_et_al_2020_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores
Marot_et_al_2020_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations
