Dataset: Carbon storage in the seagrass meadows of Gazi Bay, Kenya

Authors: Michael N. Githaiga <njoroge.michael04@gmail.com>, James G. Kairo, Linda Gilpin, Mark Huxham

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0177001

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in five separate .csv files which can be joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Githaiga_et_al_2017_materials_and_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
Githaiga_et_al_2017_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study.
Githaiga_et_al_2017_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Githaiga_et_al_2017_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Githaiga_et_al_2017_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.

Githaiga_et_al_2017_study_citations.bib and Githaiga_et_al_2017_study_citations.csv - Contain bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
