Dataset: Shear Stress, Biomass, Bulk Density, Organic Matter on the Bank of the York River, VA 2018

Authors: Gillen, M.N., T. Messerschmidt, and M.L. Kirwan

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: doi:10.6073/pasta/beed4e91c44eb7297769158f60f898d4

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. 

Gillen_et_al_2018_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
Gillen_et_al_2018_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Gillen_et_al_2018_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Gillen_et_al_2018_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.

Gillen_et_al_2018_study_citations.bib and Gillen_et_al_2018_study_citations.csv - Contain bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
