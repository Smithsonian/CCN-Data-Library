Dataset: Levee Soil Characteristics of Gloucester County, VA

Authors: Messerschmidt, T.C., ML. Kirwan and E. Hall

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.6073/pasta/e2aeeef555de4ced1f3e8676131d6850

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. 

Messerschmidt_et_al_2020_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Messerschmidt_et_al_2020_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Messerschmidt_et_al_2020_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.

Messerschmidt_et_al_2020_study_citations.bib and Messerschmidt_et_al_2020_study_citations.csv - Contain bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
