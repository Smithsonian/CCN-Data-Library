Dataset: Accretion rates and carbon sequestration in Oregon salt marshes

Authors: Cheryl A. Brown <brown.cheryl@epa.gov>, T Chris Mochon Collura, and Ted DeWitt

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.25573/serc.25024448

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in separate .csv files which can be joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Brown_et_al_2024_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
Brown_et_al_2024_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Brown_et_al_2024_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Brown_et_al_2024_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.

Brown_et_al_2024_study_citations.csv - Contains bibliographic information.
