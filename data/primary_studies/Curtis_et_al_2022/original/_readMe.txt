Dataset: Salt marsh monitoring during water years 2013 to 2019, Humboldt Bay, CA '96 water levels, surface deposition, elevation change, and carbon storage

Authors: Curtis, J.A., Thorne, K.M., Freeman C.M., Buffington, K.J., and Drexler, J.Z.

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.5066/P9QLAL7B


The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in four separate .csv files which can by joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Curtis_et_al_2022_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
Curtis_et_al_2022_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations
Curtis_et_al_2022_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores
Curtis_et_al_2022_impacts.csv - Contains anthropogenic impact information on site locations
