Dataset: Sediment Radiochemical Data from Georgia, Massachusetts, and Virginia Coastal Marshes

Authors: Everhart, C.S., Smith, C.G., Ellis, A.M., Marot, M.E., Coleman, D.J., Guntenspergen, G.R., and Kirwan, M.L.

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: https://doi.org/10.5066/P926MS6T

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. 

Everhart_et_al_2020_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Everhart_et_al_2020_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Everhart_et_al_2020_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.
Everhart_et_al_2020_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study

Everhart_et_al_2020_study_citations.bib and Everhart_et_al_2020_study_citations.csv - Contain bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
