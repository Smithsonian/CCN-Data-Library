Dataset: Soil property and geochronology (137Cs and 210Pb) data (2014) in salt marsh soils of Jamaica Bay Estuary, New York City

Authors: Wang, H. <wangh@usgs.gov>, Snedden, G., Hartig, E., and Chen, Q.

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.5066/P9XQBYXU

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in six separate .csv files which can be joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Wang_et_al_2023_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
Wang_et_al_2023_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations.
Wang_et_al_2023_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores.
Wang_et_al_2023_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations.
Wang_et_al_2023_sites.csv - Contains positional information on site locations.

Wang_et_al_2023_study_citations.bib and Wang_et_al_2023_study_citations.csv - Contain bibliographic information for publication associated with this data release.
