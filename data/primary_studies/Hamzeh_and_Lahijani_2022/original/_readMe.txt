Dataset: Soil and Vegetative Carbon Sequestration in Khuran Estuary Mangroves, Strait of Hormoz, During the Last 18 Centuries

Authors: M. A. Hamzeh and H. A. K. Lahijani

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: 10.1007/s12237-021-01037-7


The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in four separate .csv files which can by joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 


Hamzeh_and_Lahijani_2022_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study
Hamzeh_and_Lahijani_2022_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations
Hamzeh_and_Lahijani_2022_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores
Hamzeh_and_Lahijani_2022_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations
