Dataset: A Spatial Model Comparing Above- and Belowground Blue Carbon Stocks in Southwest Florida Mangroves and Salt Marshes

Authors: Kara R. Radabaugh, Ryan P. Moyer, Amanda R. Chappel, Joshua L. Breithaupt, David Lagomasino, Emma E. Dontis, Christine E. Russo, Brad E. Rosenheim, Lisa G. Chambers, Elitsa I. Peneva-Reed, Joseph M. Smoak

The data release contains tidal wetland soil carbon profiles. The data itself is housed in eight separate .csv files which can by joined by the core_id and/or site_id attributes. 

Any use of this dataset must include a citation. The DOI: https://doi.org/10.25573/serc.23960826.v1


radabaugh_et_al_2023_methods.csv - Contains information on materials and methods broken down by study 
radabaugh_et_al_2023_cores.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information on core locations
radabaugh_et_al_2023_depthseries.csv - Contains raw depth-series information for all cores
radabaugh_et_al_2023_impacts.csv - Contains anthropological impact information on site locations
radabaugh_et_al_2023_species.csv - Contains information on the dominant plant species at coring locations
radabaugh_et_al_2023_study_citations.bib - Contains bibliographic information for this data release
radabaugh_et_al_2023_study_citations.csv - Contains bibliographic information for this data release
radabaugh_et_al_2023_plant.csv - Contains information on plant species, height, and weight
radabaugh_et_al_2023_plot_summary.csv - Contains positional and descriptive information of plant plot locations 
radabaugh_et_al_2023_allometric_eq.csv - Contains information on species specific allometric equations
